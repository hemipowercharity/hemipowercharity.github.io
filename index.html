About Hemi Power

Our Mission

Hemi Power is dedicated to empowering parents and families navigating their child's hemiplegia diagnosis. We provide comprehensive information, resources, and support to help families understand and manage this condition with confidence.

What is Hemiplegia?

Hemiplegia is a condition that affects movement and strength on one side of the body. It can be present from birth (congenital) or acquired later due to injury or illness. With proper support and intervention, children with hemiplegia can lead full, active lives.

Support & Community

You're Not Alone

Receiving a hemiplegia diagnosis for your child can be overwhelming. Remember that thousands of families have walked this path before you, and many children with hemiplegia go on to achieve amazing things.

Getting Started

  • Connect with your child's medical team
  • Learn about available therapies and interventions
  • Join support groups and online communities
  • Focus on your child's abilities, not limitations
  • Celebrate small victories along the way
